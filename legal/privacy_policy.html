Privacy Policy

Photo Eraser AI — Image Cleanup Tool

Last Updated: April 10, 2025

Overview

Photo Eraser AI ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

🔒 We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal photos or images to any server. All image processing happens entirely on-device.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate the App:

  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash logs via Google Firebase Crashlytics (device model, OS version, crash stack trace — no personal data).
  • Ad Data: Google AdMob may collect ad-related data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. This applies only to free users.
  • Purchase Data: Google Play handles all in-app purchase transactions. We do not store payment information.

How Your Photos Are Processed

Photo Eraser AI uses AI-powered image processing technology to clean up and edit photos on your device:

  • All photo editing is performed entirely on your device.
  • Your photos are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Processed images are saved only to your device's photo library when you explicitly choose to save them.
  • The App does not retain copies of your photos after you close the session.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — Advertising (free users). Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — In-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • Google Firebase — Anonymous crash reporting and analytics.

Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. Anonymous crash logs are retained for 90 days to help us fix bugs, then automatically deleted. Ad identifiers are managed by Google AdMob per their policies.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, export, or delete. For ad personalization settings, you can manage these through your device's Google account settings or via the AdMob opt-out.

  • Opt out of personalized ads via Google account settings
  • Limit ad tracking via Android Settings → Privacy → Ads

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

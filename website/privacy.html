Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2025

Overview

Gemini Eraser ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

🔒 We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal photos or images to any central server. All image editing is strictly private.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate the App:

  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash logs via Google Firebase Crashlytics (device model, OS version, crash stack trace — no personal data).
  • Ad Data: Google AdMob may collect ad-related data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. This applies only to free users.
  • Purchase Data: Google Play handles all in-app purchase transactions. We do not store payment information.

How Your Photos Are Processed

Gemini Eraser uses cloud AI-powered image processing technology to clean up and edit photos:

  • Your photos are processed using a highly secure generative inference model.
  • Your photos are never stored. They are deleted immediately after the edit completes.
  • Processed images are saved only to your device's photo library when you explicitly choose to save them.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — Advertising (free users). Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — In-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • Google Firebase — Anonymous crash reporting and analytics.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
